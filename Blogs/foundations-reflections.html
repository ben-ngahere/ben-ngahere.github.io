Foundations Reflection


What are three new things you have learnt about yourself and your ego due to the Human Skills learning?

  • Use it and Improve it

    • Neural paths grow stronger the more they are used. To me and my coding it means getting better at what I already know. This could be practicing the code, reading and talking to others about their code and learning new code to improve existing knowledge

  • Time Matters

    • It takes time dependent on how complex the new behavior/skill is. To me this rings true and is one of the hardest things to want to recognise and be patient with. Whilst wanting to "know it all", learning coding is hard (if it were easy, we'd all be doing it) and recognising that it takes time will help you feel better about the progress that's made, which makes you keep going

  • Repetition Matters

    • Consistency is the key to building new pathways. Practice makes permanent. Like learning anything, practice, practice and practice is required. There's really no two ways about it and for me, recognising this gives me motivation to want to do it more. It doesn't have to be for the longest time, just consistent. Eg. 30mins everyday Vs. 3hrs one day a week - the same amount of total time is taken, but you've engaged 7 times versus the 1 time. 7 small wins versus 1 big win. Consistently achieving multiple small wins often leads to more sustained progress and motivation
