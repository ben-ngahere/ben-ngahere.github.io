Explain a situation where you have made an ethical decision. Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made, and how you reflect on the decision now

In my previous Retail life, I was met a couple times with situations where ethics vs. business policy (in some cases retail law) came about. Most of these times occured when customers were wanting to return an item. Now bluntly, returning an item due to change of mind generally isn't an accepted reason for return. This was the decision that had to be made almost everytime. The one time I didn't go this way and actually gave the return was because of the situation the customer had found themselves in. This has got nothing to do with our policy but I made the decision because it was the right thing to do and Integrity to me is everything. The customer was genuine and the product was un-opened and a popular item that we could have easily resold (which we did later that week). I got a slight repremand from my boss for not follwing policy, but he had to say that in order to "tell me off". He too would have done the same thing so I've never felt that this was incorrect, even if my boss didn't agree, I still would stand by what I did

Describe how your culture and the people around you have influenced your values and identity

I come from a rather large family (I have 7 siblings) and alot of them live locally. My parental dynamic saw my mum raise us the most whilst working and dad doing long hours with the truck driving - he'd leave very early in the morning and not get in until after we'd be alseep. We spent alot of time at my grandparents (mums parents) house and this would be where alot of old school lessons were taught. Mum had somewhat of a christian upbringing and this has influenced alot of how I am today too. Whilst not religious, I'm greatful for being taught these kinds of lessons. Being Maori and Niuean has also influenced me massively. Biggest lesson I learnt here was to give, even when you can't and not placing too much value on material things
